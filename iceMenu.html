                      Coconuts grow on  trees in tropical 
                      climates.The fruit of the coconut
                      palm, coconuts are round nuts that
                      start life green in color and turn
                      brown as they ripen.To eat a coconut,
                      you must break the seed in half.Inside 
                      a ripe coconut, you'll find fresh coconut 
                      meat and coconut milk.Both  the meat  
                      and the milk of the coconut seed are
                      rich in a wide variety of vitamins and 
                      nutrients.
