Elvis Presley - concert of imitation

The big event "Presley Imitation" will hold at the Elvis presley`s hometown - Memphis, Tennessee. There is an age requirment for the participants - the minimum age is at least 20 and the maximum is 50. They need to be dressed with an original costume of Elvis Presley. During the competition everyone of the participants will singa song by their choice from Elvis music art.

The concert start at 19:00 o`clock and will last two days. The first thing you need to do is to check out your age and if it is enough make a registration / on the next page/. The jury will choose two competitors who are the best Elvis Presley`s imitators. It means theht there will be one first and one second place.

Our jury concsists ot 3 members - Lisa Marie Predsley / Elvis Presley`s daughter/, Tom Jones / a colleague and friend of Presley / and Morgan Freeman - an actor who will watch for a good acting.

If you want to participate in the competetition make registration on the next page.
